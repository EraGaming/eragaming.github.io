Our Journey
from 2018
to Today

Our Humble Beginnings

It all started in 2018 with a vision to bring gamers together. Endless ERA Gaming was born out of a small Discord community, a digital gathering place for gamers to share, play, and discuss their favorite games. Our dedication to creating a welcoming and engaging community set the foundation for what was to come.

A Milestone Year: 2022

Our journey took a significant leap forward in 2022 when we partnered with Otafest to enhance their gaming hall. This collaboration not only improved the gaming experience for attendees but also marked our entry into the world of large-scale gaming event management. Our successful work at Otafest opened the doors to another prestigious opportunity - Animethon. Here, we were tasked with presenting tournaments and operating the video wall, a testament to our expertise and growing reputation in the gaming community.

Continued Growth in 2023

In 2023, we continued to expand our horizons. Our role at Otafest evolved from enhancing the gaming hall to comprehensive floor planning and stage management, ensuring an unparalleled gaming experience for all attendees. At Animethon, we took on the management of the entertainment stage, further solidifying our position as leaders in gaming event management.
Our Services Today
Today, Endless ERA Gaming stands as a beacon for quality and innovation in gaming event services. From meticulous floor and stage planning to complete management of gaming and entertainment stages, our portfolio reflects our growth and the trust the gaming community places in us. Our expertise encompasses:

Event Services

Tailored solutions for gaming tournaments, including planning, execution, and post-event analysis.

Broadcast Production

Elevating gaming events with professional live streaming and video production.

Consulting Services

Offering strategic insights for event planning, leveraging our experience to help clients achieve their vision.
Looking Ahead
As we look to the future, Endless ERA Gaming remains committed to enriching the gaming community, fostering connections, and delivering exceptional gaming experiences. Our journey from a small Discord community to a key player in gaming event management is just the beginning.
We invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Whether you’re a gamer, event organizer, content creator, or sponsor, there’s a place for you in the Endless ERA Gaming community. Together, let's continue to shape the future of gaming events.
